Weddings

Weddings are my specialty! I believe in helping you to create the wedding of your dreams. I provide the music and sound system for your ceremony as well as your reception. Whether it’s elegantly formal or a wild party that you desire I will do my best to customize the entertainment to your wishes. I will keep the event flowing smooth from beginning to end by helping with introductions and announcements. I will also help coordinate the event with your other vendors and wedding professionals such as dinner, cake cutting, toasts and special dances. I will play the music you and your guests want to hear, and if there is something you do not want played simply tell me and I will gladly accommodate. You can choose all or some of your music or let me choose the music that will get your guests on the dance floor, either way I will work hard to ensure your event is a success. Why DJ Peedee?

  • Master of Ceremony – I will be responsible for making formal introductions and announcements, while coordinating the timing, and insuring that everything is organized and handled properly so you can relax and enjoy yourself.
  • State of the Art Sound Systems and Professional Grade Equipment - I use the best names in the DJ Industry.
  • Play Your Choice of Music – You can submit a most play list, a maybe play list and even a do not play list.
  • Formally dressed DJ - I will be professionally dressed unless you would like me to wear business casual attire.
  • Wireless Microphones - Great for toast and announcements.
  • Set up and Break down time included - I will arrive one hour prior to playing time. Usually it takes a half hour to set up but I like to arrive early just in case. You are only charged for hours of music played and each additional hour that you book is completely refundable if you don’t use them.
  • Flexible Reservations: No fee or price increases if you need to change your location (within 35 miles of the city), times or even your date!
  • EXCELLENT Customer Service – I take pride in treating my customers like family!
  • Price – My combination of quality and price make me the best value in the DJ business. Guaranteed! I do not believe you don’t have to pay an arm and a leg for a great DJ!
